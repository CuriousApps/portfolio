About Me

This is me.

Hello! My name is Jacob Voyles, I am orginally from southern IL. I moved out to the Bay Area around 1 year ago, since then I have been working at a startup company in Fremont, CA. I don’t really have a specific job title in this company since it’s a startup… I do everything. I was originally hired as a front-end developer, but then I started to work on an iOS application, then an android Application, etc…

I love the Bay Area! Everywhere I go I am able to talk to someone about software or see something about software. It’s a very big change, being from a small town with 6K people.

Some of my favorite pass times are video games, music and cars. My favorite video games are CS:GO and Dota 2 (League sucks…). I also really like music, my favorite artist are Kendrick Lamar, Drake, Future and Yung Lean. I really enjoy cars too… my first time coming out to the bay area i was amazed… I never saw exotic cars before. It was a surreal experience seeing one of my favorite cars in real life, a ‘Lamborghini Aventador’. Some of my favorite cars are Nissan GTR, Tesla Model S P90D, Lamborghini Aventador and Pagani Zonda.
